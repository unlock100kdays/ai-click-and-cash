Anti-Spam Policy

Last updated: June 2025

AI Click And Cash is committed to communicating with you only in ways you have explicitly consented to. We take spam very seriously and comply fully with the CAN-SPAM Act and applicable email marketing laws.

Our Commitment

We will never:

  • Send you unsolicited commercial emails without your permission
  • Use deceptive subject lines or misleading sender information
  • Rent, sell, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Continue emailing you after you have unsubscribed

How We Communicate

We only send emails to individuals who have:

  • Opted in through a form on our website
  • Made a purchase from us (transactional and follow-up emails)
  • Otherwise given us explicit permission to contact them

Unsubscribe

Every marketing email we send includes a clear and functional unsubscribe link at the bottom. You can opt out at any time by clicking that link. Once you unsubscribe, we will remove you from our marketing list within 10 business days.

If you experience any issues unsubscribing, please contact us directly at support@aiclickandcash.com and we will remove you manually.

Reporting Spam

If you believe you have received an unsolicited email from us, or if someone is misusing our brand name to send spam, please report it to us immediately at support@aiclickandcash.com. We take these reports seriously and will investigate promptly.

CAN-SPAM Compliance

All of our commercial emails comply with the CAN-SPAM Act by:

  • Including our physical mailing address
  • Using honest subject lines
  • Identifying the message as an advertisement where required
  • Providing a clear opt-out mechanism
  • Honoring opt-out requests promptly
